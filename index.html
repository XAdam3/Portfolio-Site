Who I am

Graphic Designer & Developer based out of LV

My name is Adam Assalimy. I'm American b. 1989 Manhattan, Kansas. I began my major in Graphic Design at the School of the Art Institute of Chicago and completed my Bachelor of Arts in Graphic Design at Oakland University. I chose to go into Graphic Design because I’m attracted to the concept of fusing art and technology to communicate a message using computers.Computers can be helpful and frustrating at times, for that reason, I view my Apple MacBook as another tool to use for my artwork, such as a pencil or a ruler. I’ve made many visual illustrations and created many hand-made artworks. I believe the answer lies within understanding the question. I enjoy challenging myself, I go through what I call a planning process; creating rough-draft models, thus, having a physical image of what the piece would look like, then evaluating the sample from all angles. When I’ve completed the planning process then I can move on to the final step. By doing so, I have allowed myself to gain further insight, due to analyzing the sample from different point of views, by progress through discovery. After the planning process, I start using the computer and other essential tools to execute my desired product. In art, I view myself as a Modernist, a Cubist, and a Surrealist thinker with the philosophy of not being concerned for the future but rather present moment. Therefore, I put all my time and energy into the task at hand in the present moment because I know I’ll get what I put into it.
